§ Abstract 要旨

Pending is eight preceding papers describe the shape of a keyhole. This ninth paper is the key — but the key is not yet cut. We present what can be said about the key's teeth from the keyhole's inner geometry, and mark explicitly what must remain pending until the object is held in the hand. The paper is an honest draft; its incompleteness is structural, not negligent.

"The key is inferred from the keyhole, but not yet cut. The incompleteness is structural." 鍵は鍵穴から推論できるが、まだ切られていない。この未完は構造的である。

§ Key findings 主要な発見
01

The keyhole's inner geometry is knowable.

From Papers I–VIII, we can specify the negative space the key must fill: arrow, ring, mountain, prism, helix, radar, torus, triangle.

鍵穴の内形は既知 — 八論文の負空間として定まる。

02

The key itself remains pending.

What metal, what hand, what turn — these cannot yet be specified without equivocation. We mark them as (pending) rather than invent.

鍵そのものは未定 — 金属・手・回転は未だ仮置きのみ。

03

Honest incompleteness beats false closure.

Publishing the draft key with explicit (pending) markers is strictly better than forcing closure; the draft invites correction.

虚偽の完結より正直な未完の方が良い。
